Our Story

About Appaloosa

Born from a love of considered design

The Beginning

Clothing as a quiet statement

Appaloosa was founded in Chișinău with a singular conviction: that the clothes we wear every day should be made with as much care as they are worn. We set out to build a wardrobe — not a trend — partnering with artisans across Portugal, Italy, and Eastern Europe.

Craft & Care

Why we do what we do

Each season, we travel to our partner ateliers to see the process firsthand. We touch the fabrics, meet the makers, and ask the hard questions about sourcing and sustainability.

We believe in slow fashion not as a buzzword, but as a practice. When you buy from Appaloosa, you are investing in something made to last — something that will still feel right five years from now.

The name Appaloosa comes from the American spotted horse — a creature known for its independence, beauty, and surprising resilience. These are the qualities we build into every piece.

What we stand for

Our Values

01

Craft over Speed

We work with ateliers that take the time to do things properly. No rushed production lines. No corner-cutting. Only skilled hands and thoughtful process.

02

Materials that Matter

Linen from Belgium, cotton from Portugal, silk from Como. We source deliberately, prioritising natural fibres, certified farms, and traceable supply chains.

03

Longevity by Design

Our silhouettes are timeless because we design them to be. Each piece is considered not for a season, but for a decade — and beyond if you care for it well.
