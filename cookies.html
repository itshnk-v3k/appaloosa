What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our site and to understand how you use it. Below is a summary of the types of cookies we use.

TypePurposeDuration
EssentialRequired for the shop to function (cart, session)Session
FunctionalRemember your language and currency preferences1 year
AnalyticsUnderstand how visitors use our site (anonymised)2 years
MarketingShow relevant ads on other platforms (only if consented)90 days

Managing Your Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website — for example, your cart may not save between sessions.

Most browsers allow you to: view cookies that have been set, block cookies from specific sites, block all third-party cookies, clear all cookies when you close your browser.

Third-Party Cookies

Some features on our site use third-party services (such as payment providers and analytics platforms) which may set their own cookies. These are governed by the respective third party's privacy policies.
